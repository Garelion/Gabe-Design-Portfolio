Island Explorers

Play on itch.io

Systems Designer

Island Explorers is a 2D top-down light survival game, developed by a team of three, myself included, for a class assignment during my Bachelor's at Full Sail.

Designed and Implemented:

  • Inventory system
  • Crafting system
  • Player controls and animations

Scraps Level

Level Designer

Scraps was a project made for students to build and add onto, so they could learn to make levels from conception, to blockout, to implementation of mechanics. All assets are original, except the playable character.

Marble Game Level

Designer / Programmer

The Marble Game was a team project at Full Sail, where each member was responsible for designing and implementing their own level while also contributing to shared mechanics and systems. I focused on developing the player controls, creating the collectible system, and building one of the levels. The entire project was completed within a one-week timeframe.

Simple Tanks

Designer / Programmer

Simple Tanks is a game created in a one-day game jam. It's a game where up to 2 players can fight each other in tanks from a top-down 2.5D perspective. In this project, I was responsible for the tank controls and the level designs.

Modeled Scene

3D Artist (Maya)

This scene was created as part of an early coursework project, where students were tasked with modeling individual assets and assembling them into a cohesive environment. The entire piece was developed in Maya during the first months of my Bachelor’s program. To make the scene more interactive, I included a “Find the Spongebobs” minigame, inviting viewers to spot all the hidden Spongebobs throughout the environment.

Gex Lair

3D Artist (Unity)

The Gex Lair Model was a project where students were tasked with recreating a scene from their favorite franchises in Unity. While not an exact replica, the final result closely captures the look and feel of the original.
